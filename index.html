Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Why Play?

In this day and age, most people spend hours on their cell phones, aimlessly clicking on links and browsing through social media sites. None of those activities can increase your focus, improve your short-term memory, or help you think faster--but playing this memory game can!
